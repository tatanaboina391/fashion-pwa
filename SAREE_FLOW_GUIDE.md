# Saree Customization Flow - Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 How to Use the Saree Customization Flow

### Step 1: Access the Flow
1. Open the application in your browser
2. Click on the **Saree** category card on the home page
3. You'll be redirected to the Type Selection page

---

### Step 2: Select Blouse & Saree Types
**What you'll see:**
- Two main sections: Blouse Selection and Saree Selection
- For Blouse: Choose both Front Neck and Back Neck designs
- For Saree: Browse categories (Traditional, Silk, Cotton, Designer, Occasion) and select a type

**How to select:**
- Click on any card to select it
- Selected cards will show a checkmark (✓)
- Your selections appear in the summary section below

**When you're ready:**
- The "Create Saree" button will enable once you've selected:
  - ✅ One Front Neck design
  - ✅ One Back Neck design
  - ✅ One Saree type
- Click "Create Saree →" to continue

---

### Step 3: View Pencil Sketch
**What you'll see:**
- A pencil sketch preview of your design
- Your selections displayed at the top
- Design feature cards showing your choices

**What to do:**
- Review the sketch
- If you want to change selections, click "← Change Selection"
- If you're happy, click "Continue to Customize Colors →"

---

### Step 4: Customize Colors
**What you'll see:**
- Interactive preview of blouse and saree
- Color palette section (initially shows a placeholder)

**How to customize:**
1. **Click on the blouse preview** → Blouse color palette appears
2. **Select a color** → Color applies to blouse, palette closes
3. **Click on the saree preview** → Saree color palette appears
4. **Select a color** → Color applies to saree, palette closes

**When you're ready:**
- Once both colors are selected, the "View 360° Preview →" button enables
- Click it to see the 360° view

---

### Step 5: View 360° Rotation
**What you'll see:**
- Your complete design with selected colors
- A 3D-style rotatable model
- Control panel with rotation options

**How to interact:**
1. **Auto-rotation**: The model rotates automatically by default
2. **Drag to rotate**: Click and drag the model to rotate manually
3. **Use the slider**: Drag the slider to set a specific rotation angle (0-360°)
4. **Pause/Resume**: Click "⏸️ Pause" or "▶️ Auto Rotate" to control auto-rotation
5. **Reset**: Click "🔄 Reset View" to return to front view (0°)

**View indicators:**
- The view angle indicator shows: Front View, Side View (Right), Back View, or Side View (Left)
- The rotation degree is displayed (e.g., "45°")

**When you're done:**
- Click "← Back to Home" to return to the home page
- Click "Create New Design" to start a new saree customization

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Q: The "Create Saree" button is disabled**
A: Make sure you've selected all three required items:
   - Front neck design
   - Back neck design
   - Saree type

**Q: The "View 360°" button is disabled**
A: You need to select both blouse color and saree color first

**Q: The page is blank after clicking a link**
A: This might happen if you navigate directly to a URL. Always start from the home page and click the Saree card

**Q: The 360° rotation isn't working**
A: Try clicking "🔄 Reset View" or refresh the page. Make sure your browser supports CSS 3D transforms
